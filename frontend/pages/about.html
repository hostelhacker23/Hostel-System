About Our Hostel Management System

Our project is designed to streamline hostel operations through a digital platform that enables secure student registration, room allocation, fee tracking, and staff management — all from a user-friendly dashboard.

  • 📌 Simplifies hostel admissions with an online registration system for students.
  • 📌 Automates room allocation based on availability and student preferences.
  • 📌 Tracks student attendance and fee payments efficiently.
  • 📌 Offers admin, student, and warden dashboards with role-based access.
  • 📌 Reduces paperwork, improves transparency, and enhances overall hostel management.
  • 📌 Built with modern web technologies for better performance and user experience.
